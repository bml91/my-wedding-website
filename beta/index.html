About Us

Our journey together started by the end of 2020, taking a for Ulli surprising turn when Bogdan planned a romantic proposal on the Maldives on 17th of January 2024. A bit more than a year and one sabbatical later, we are getting married and want to celebrate this beautiful day together with you, our most important people! We love you, thank you for being part of our lives. Our biggest wish and priority for this day is to hopefully successfully create an atmosphere in which everybody has a wonderful time, feels like home and safe to fully be themselves.

Schloss Saareck

Castle Saareck is located in Mettlach right at the riverbank of river Saar ánd was built in 1902 in historicism style by family Boch. Yes, it’s the Boch from Villeroy and Boch. Even today you can find the company Villeroy and Boch on the other side of the river. Up to the middle of the second world war, family Boch was living in the castle, then remodeling it to a hospital for the wounded, which saved it from being destroyed during the war. Today the company Villeroy & Boch is owning the castle and is using it as a hotel and venue for festivities.

Getting There

For most of you it is probably easiest to fly in via Luxembourg airport and then take a taxi or train to the castle or hotel, depending on where you will be staying. Please feel free to reach out in case of any questions.
We intend to plan a get together day before the wedding, so we can already spend some time with you and so you can already get to know some of the other guests and feel a bit more relaxed on the day of the wedding as hopefully not too many faces will be new for you. So depending on when you arrive and what you feel like, please let us know if you are interested in joining us for a fun day before the wedding.

Gifts

You really do not need to bring us gifts. We know that most of you have a long journey to be a part of it, so our biggest gift is being able to celebrate together with you! In case you still want to bring something, we would like to kindly ask you to refrain from buying “random stuff” and instead either share some coin for our honeymoon or if you would really like to hand over a physical gift to pick one from our wish list.

Any activity or game you might want to plan must be aligned with our wedding planner Jenny in advance. We kindly urge you to refrain from preparing activities like “here is a block of concrete with coins in it, now use the hammer for an hour to get them” - we all want to have fun and a good time with things we feel comfortable with. Thank you for understanding. .
